Our Mission

To break the walls down in a foster child’s life. To be a positive force and provide a brighter future for the child.

Our Vision

Our communities, business and neighborhoods will be inspired to break down the walls of a child through kindness and inspiration. We will positively impact a child’s life throughout their care and after care in the foster system by raising money throughout our communities to help pay for services and gaps that are not provided for by the state.

Our Goal

To raise awareness and funds to assist children in the Arizona Foster Care System and those who have aged out of the foster system, with educational, extracurricular and person growth needs that are not met with the funding provided for these children by the State of Arizona.
