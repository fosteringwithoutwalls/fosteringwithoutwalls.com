Donations Matter

These donations are critical for supporting kids in the foster care system in the state of Arizona. As of October 2019 there are 13,476 children in out-of-home foster care (source: Arizona Department of Child Safety Monthly Operational and Outcome Report Nov 2019). The money you donate will help them with clothing, participation in activists after school, Christmas gifts, summer camps, and so much more. Fosterin Without Walls is committed to providing a brighter future for these kid so that one day they will be productive members of our community.
